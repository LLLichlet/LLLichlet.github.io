1.3 Dimension

Definition 1.3.1.

Let V be a vector space over a field F. A subspace chain in V is a finite sequence of subspaces

{0}=V0⊊V1⊊⋯⊊Vk⊆V.
Definition 1.3.2.

For a subspace U⊆V, the height of U is

ht⁡(U)=sup{k∣{0}=V0⊊⋯⊊Vk=U⁢ is a subspace chain}.

If the supremum does not exist, ht⁡(U)=∞.

Definition 1.3.3.

The dimension of V is dimV=ht⁡(V).
